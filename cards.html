The card is
the link.

The prompt card is what a taker receives when they lift an object from a cubby. It connects this exchange to the one before it. Without it, the relay is a bookcrossing box. With it, the relay is a chain.

An object, not a screen

The card is the only piece of the relay a person carries away. It travels in a pocket, gets pinned to a wall, becomes the thing that pulls someone back.

English on the front, Italian on the back. No project name, no logo. The card is the prompt, nothing else.

85 × 55 mm · cream stock · card number set small for the archive

The set

Ten of the fifty-odd cards in circulation. Tap or click any card to read the Italian on its back.

Production

How the set is made, kept, and renewed.
Format
85 × 55 mm, cream stock. The card number is printed small for archive cross-reference, not for the user to act on.
Quantity
Fifty to sixty unique cards live at once. About ten are retired and ten introduced each term.
Authoring
The first round is the designer's. Later rounds are co-written with students, or drawn from previous chain submissions: a question someone left becomes next term's card.
Not on the card
No project name, no marks, no branding. The card is the prompt. Branding lives on the cabinet.
